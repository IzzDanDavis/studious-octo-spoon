*create hero_name ""
*create strength 50
*create magic 50
*create wisdom 50

Epic Quest - A Fantasy Adventure

Welcome, brave hero!

What is your name?
*set hero_name "Adventurer"

Greetings, ${hero_name}!

You stand at the gates of the ancient kingdom.
The king has summoned you for a dangerous quest.

Choose your character class:

*choice
    #Warrior - Master of Strength
        *set strength 80
        *set magic 30
        *set wisdom 40
        You are a mighty WARRIOR!
        Your muscles ripple with power.
        
    #Wizard - Master of Magic
        *set strength 30
        *set magic 80
        *set wisdom 60
        You are a powerful WIZARD!
        Arcane energy flows through you.
        
    #Paladin - Master of Balance
        *set strength 60
        *set magic 60
        *set wisdom 70
        You are a noble PALADIN!
        You balance strength and magic with wisdom.

The king speaks:
"${hero_name}, the dragon has stolen our kingdom's treasure!
Will you help us?"

*choice
    #Yes, I will help!
        "Brave ${hero_name}! Take this sword and go!"
        *set strength 90
        You receive a legendary sword!
        
    #I need more information first
        "Wise choice, ${hero_name}. The dragon lives in the north mountains."
        *set wisdom 80
        Your wisdom increases from asking questions!
        
    #I want a reward first
        "Very well, here are 100 gold coins in advance."
        You receive gold but the king looks disappointed.

Your Character Stats:

*stat_chart
    text hero_name
    percent strength
    percent magic
    percent wisdom

Your epic quest begins!

To be continued...

*finish
